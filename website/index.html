Airplane Simulator

Written and Developed by Jordan Mason - Drury University

Project Description:
Determine the most efficient method for passengers to board an airplane in terms of time and ease of implementation. The data was generated from a program designed to factor in multiple variables and human behavior characteristics to generate results that are as accurate as possible. Below are different simulations for each method that was tested, clicking on one will launch that simulation.
